About the Artist

Information about the artist and the story behind the artworks.

Johannes Vermeer was a Dutch Baroque Period painter who specialized in domestic interior scenes of middle-class life. He is considered one of the greatest painters of the Dutch Golden Age. During his lifetime, he was a moderately successful provincial genre painter, recognized in Delft and The Hague.

FAQ

Answers to common questions about purchasing, shipping, and returns.

How do I purchase an artwork?

Select the artwork you wish to purchase and click on 'View Details'. Then, follow the instructions to complete your purchase.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

How is shipping calculated?

Shipping costs are calculated based on the size, weight, and destination of the artwork. The total cost will be displayed at checkout.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on the destination. Typically, it takes 5-10 business days for domestic orders and 10-20 business days for international orders.

Can I track my order?

Yes, you will receive a tracking number once your order has been shipped.

What is the return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. The artwork must be returned in its original condition. Please contact us for return instructions.

What if the artwork arrives damaged?

If your artwork arrives damaged, please contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We will arrange for a replacement or refund.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team through the contact form on our website or by emailing support@artgallery.com.
